Airport

Frederick Douglass Greater Rochester International Airport (ROC) is the only airport in Rochester and is only 20 minutes from the wedding venue.

Alternatively, Buffalo Niagara International Airport (BUF) is 1 hour and 15 minutes from Rochester.

Getting Around

There are all of the usal Rental Car Companies available at ROC: Avis, Budget, Dollar, Enterprise, Hertz, National. Uber and Lyft are also available at the airport and around Rochester.
